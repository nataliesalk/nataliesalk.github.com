Natalie Salk

Natalie Salk is a senior Mechanical Engineer at Tufts University with a concentration in entreprenurial leadership and a minor in Astrophysics. She is also an active member of Alpha Tau Omega of Massachusetts. Most of Natalie's time is dedicated to Captaining Tufts University Varsity sailing team where she has recieved four varsity letters.She has also recieved honors nationally as an Honerable Mention All-American and within her confrence as NEISA Sportswoman of the year. In her free time Natalie enjoys listening to music, dancing and playing sports.
