Robokeys

The Piano playing robot

Tufts University, Medford, MA

This was a project that I put together with two other mechanical engineers and a electrical engineer at Tufts University. The robot was actuated by ten solenoids and two stepper motors. We developed a program to read in a MIDI file so our hands could play any song. We ran into some difficulties with the electrial engineering in the project. In the end our power supplies could not provide enough current to actuate the hands back and forth so we played this diddy and sang along.
